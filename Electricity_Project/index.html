Analysis On Electricity Consumption In India

India is one of the leading producers and consumers of electricity in the world. This project analyzes electricity consumption patterns across Indian states using data analytics, machine learning, and interactive visualization.

Electricity Consumption Dashboard

⚡ History of Electricity Consumption in India

Electricity consumption in India began in the early 20th century mainly for urban lighting and industrial use. After independence, expansion of power generation and rural electrification programs significantly increased electricity demand.

Economic reforms in the 1990s accelerated industrial development and energy usage across different sectors. With growing population, urbanization, and increased use of appliances, electricity demand continues to rise across India.
